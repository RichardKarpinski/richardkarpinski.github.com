Office Location

Our office is located on the southwest corner of Seventh Avenue and Central Park South (59th Street). It is easily reached by public transportation: the buses and subway lines which service the transportation hub at Columbus Circle stop one block away. Other subway lines running under Sixth and Seventh Avenues have stops one or two blocks away. Several bus lines have stops immediately adjacent to the office. Although driving to midtown may be an adventure, there are several parking garages in the neighborhood. Metered street parking is extremely limited.

Directions by Subway

Take any of the following subway lines to the indicated stop and follow the walking directions to our office:

  • N, W, Q or R to 57th Street Station (7th Avenue and 57th Street).
    Walk north to 7th Avenue and 59th Street (same as Central Park South).
  • A, C, D, 1 or B to Columbus Circle Station.
    Walk one block east to 7th Avenue along Central Park South.
  • F to 57th Street Station (and 6th Avenue and 57th Street).
    Walk north two blocks to Central Park South, then one block west along Central Park South to 7th Avenue.
  • E to 7th Avenue Station (53rd Street between Broadway and 7th Avenue).
    Walk along 7th Avenue 6 blocks north to Central Park South.

The Metropolitan Transit Authority provides a map of the New York City subway system, which you might find helpful. It is also available in PDF format, which is better for printing and viewing details, but which may take several minutes to download on a dial-up connection.

Directions by Bus

Take any of the following bus lines to the indicated stop and follow the walking directions to our office:

  • M5 or M7 to 7th Avenue and Central Park South.
  • M10, M20 or M104 to Columbus Circle.
    Walk one block east to 7th Avenue along Central Park South.
  • M6 to (the end of its run) at Central Park South (between 6th and 7th Avenues).
    Walk half a block west to 7th Avenue.
  • M30, M31 or M57 to 7th Avenue.
    Walk 2 blocks north along 7th Avenue to Central Park South.
  • Q32, M11, M1, M2, M3, M4, M101, M102, M103, M98 or M15 to 57th Street.
    Transfer to M30, M31 or M57 to 7th Avenue.
    Walk 2 blocks north along 7th Avenue to Central Park South.

Directions by Car

From East Side (below 60th Street):

  • any avenue north to 60th Street,
  • left on 60th Street,
  • right at the Plaza Hotel onto Central Park South,
  • west to 7th Avenue.

From Upper East Side:

  • any avenue south to 60th Street,
  • right on 60th Street,
  • right again at the Plaza Hotel onto Central Park South,
  • west to 7th Avenue.

From Midtown:

  • north on 6th Avenue to Central Park,
  • left on Central Park South,
  • west to 7th Avenue

     or

  • north on 8th Avenue to Central Park,
  • right at Columbus Circle on Central Park South,
  • east to 7th Avenue

From Upper West Side:

  • Broadway to Columbus Circle,
  • east along Central Park South to 7th avenue,

     or

  • 9th or 11th Avenues south to 58th Street,
  • east to Columbus Circle,
  • east along Central Park South to 7th Avenue.

From West Side (below 58th Street):

  • 8th, 10th or 11th Avenue north to 58th street,
  • east on 58th to Columbus Circle,
  • east along Central Park South to 7th Avenue.

From George Washington Bridge:

  • Henry Hudson Parkway (9A) south to 56th/57th Street Exit,
  • east on 56th Street to 8th Avenue,
  • left onto 8th Avenue,
  • north to Columbus Circle,
  • east along Central Park South to 7th Avenue.

From Lincoln Tunnel:

  • any avenue north to 58th Street,
  • east on 56th Street to 8th Avenue,
  • left onto 8th Avenue,
  • north to Columbus Circle,
  • east along Central Park South to 7th Avenue.

From Triborough Bridge:

  • FDR Drive south to 63rd Street Exit,
  • straight west on 63rd Street to 5th Avenue,
  • left on 5th Avenue to 60th Street,
  • right on 60th Street,
  • right again at the Plaza Hotel onto Central Park South, west to 7th Avenue.

From Queensborough Bridge:

  • straight ahead onto 60th Street west to its end beyond 5th Avenue,
  • right at the Plaza Hotel onto Central Park South, west to 7th Avenue.
