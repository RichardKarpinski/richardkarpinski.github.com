Description of Office

Our Offices occupy a ground-level suite in a large residential/professional building, situated on the southwest corner of Seventh Avenue and Central Park South. Entry is through a separate (non-lobby) entrance facing the New York Athletic Club across Seventh Avenue. Although there is a single step up to the entrance, limited wheelchair access is available via a portable ramp.

The cozy space, which echoes the round contours of the building, features:

  • Design: Georges Knafo, AIA
  • Interior Design: Jeffrey J. Wittenstein, AIA
  • Artwork: Mario Torroella

Operating Facilities

Comfortable, clean, in-office surgical rooms:

  • for local anesthesia procedures
  • from mole removal to liposuction and blepharoplasty
For procedures requiring an anesthesiologist for sedation or general anesthesia:

  • an office surgery suite nearby on Central Park West
  • full accreditation by AAAAS,
  • modern, well-maintained anesthesia equipment,
  • state-of-the-art monitoring and operating instruments,
  • anesthesiologist certified by ABA,
  • PAs and RNs certified by ASPSN.
For larger procedures or higher risk patients:

  • outpatient ambulatory surgery at The Roosevelt Hospital
  • an Academic Affiliate of Columbia University College of Physicians and Surgeons
  • a 505-bed, full-service community and tertiary care hospital
